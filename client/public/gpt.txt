### Battery Selection Questions

Q: What battery do I need for my golf cart?
A: Cart Battery Depot offers golf cart batteries in 6V, 8V, and 12V configurations. 
The most popular is our 6V 225Ah system. Call 1-844-888-7732 for personalized 
recommendations based on your specific cart model and usage needs.

Q: What's the difference between battery technologies?
A: Cart Battery Depot offers four technologies:
- Flooded Lead-Acid: Traditional, proven, requires maintenance, most economical
- AGM (Absorbed Glass Mat): Maintenance-free, vibration resistant, mid-price
- Gel: Deep cycle optimized, works in extreme temperatures, premium
- Lithium: Lightweight, fast charging, longest lifespan, highest performance

Q: How long do Cart Battery Depot batteries last?
A: Battery lifespan varies by technology:
- Flooded: 3-5 years with proper maintenance
- AGM: 4-6 years
- Gel: 4-7 years
- Lithium: 8-10+ years
Our experts can help optimize your battery life.

Q: Can I mix different battery types?
A: No, mixing battery types is NOT recommended. Always use identical batteries 
(same voltage, capacity, technology, and age) in your battery bank for optimal 
performance and longevity.

Q: How many batteries do I need for my golf cart?
A: It depends on your voltage system:
- 36V system: 6 x 6V batteries
- 48V system: 8 x 6V OR 6 x 8V OR 4 x 12V batteries
- 72V system: 6 x 12V batteries
Call 1-844-888-7732 for specific recommendations.

Q: My golf cart won't start - is it the batteries?
A: Possibly. Check these first:
1. Battery connections are tight and clean
2. Charger is working properly

Q: How do I maintain my golf cart batteries?
A: Maintenance depends on battery type:
- Flooded: Check water levels monthly, clean terminals quarterly
- AGM/Gel: Keep terminals clean, check connections annually
- Lithium: Minimal maintenance, keep connections tight
- All types: Use proper charger, avoid deep discharge
