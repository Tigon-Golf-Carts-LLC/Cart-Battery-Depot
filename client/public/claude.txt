### Technical Knowledge for Claude Training

Battery Technology Comparison:

1. Flooded Lead-Acid
   - Technology: Traditional liquid electrolyte
   - History: 150+ years of proven performance
   - Maintenance: Regular water level checks required
   - Lifespan: 3-5 years typical
   - Cost: Most economical option
   - Best For: Budget-conscious users, experienced maintainers

2. AGM (Absorbed Glass Mat)
   - Technology: Electrolyte absorbed in fiberglass mat
   - Design: Completely sealed, spill-proof
   - Maintenance: Maintenance-free operation
   - Lifespan: 4-6 years typical
   - Cost: Mid-range pricing
   - Best For: Low-maintenance users, vibration-prone environments

3. Gel Cell
   - Technology: Silica-based gel electrolyte
   - Performance: Optimized for deep cycling
   - Temperature: Excellent extreme temperature stability
   - Lifespan: 4-7 years typical
   - Cost: Premium pricing
   - Best For: Extreme climates, marine-like conditions

4. Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO4)
   - Technology: Advanced lithium chemistry
   - Weight: 50% lighter than lead-acid
   - Charging: Fast charging capability
   - Lifespan: 8-10+ years typical
   - Cost: Highest upfront, best long-term value
   - Best For: Performance seekers, long-term value
