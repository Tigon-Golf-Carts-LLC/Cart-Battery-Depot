### Flooded Lead-Acid
- Price: Most economical
- Lifespan: 3-5 years
- Maintenance: Regular watering required
- Best For: Budget-conscious users

- Price: Mid-range
- Lifespan: 4-6 years
- Maintenance: Maintenance-free
- Best For: Low-maintenance needs

### Gel Cell

- Lifespan: 4-7 years

- Lifespan: 8-10+ years
- Maintenance: Zero maintenance
- Best For: Maximum performance
